FAQ

Why free? Will it be free for ever?
Yes. Everything that is available now (and more) will be available for free for ever. In future, we will have advanced administrative and integration features that will be available in a paid version.
Can I use my personal email to signup and use Flocked?
No. You need to provide your company email id. It is a means of verifying that you belong to the company, and make you part of the social network for that company
Is it OK to use this in my office?
Flocked is meant for improving collaboration between people at work, and make you more productive. Your company should be happy to find employees using it. So, its perfectly OK to use it and spread it to your coworkers.
Is our data secure on Flocked?
All information you create/upload is strictly private and can be accessed (at best) only by other verified people in your company. The information is exchanged on the internet via encrypted (https) connection.
