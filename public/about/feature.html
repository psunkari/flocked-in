Take a Tour of flocked.in

Flocked-In combines the concepts of micro blogging, social networking, messaging and sharing, into a single platform. It is similar in user-interface and ease of use, with consumer products like Facebook, LinkedIn? or Twitter. It essentially solves the pain of email overload at work, and breaks down information silos by facilitating free information flow across departments and hierarchy.
