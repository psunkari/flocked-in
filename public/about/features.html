What is Flocked-in?

Flocked-in integrates micro-blogging, user profiles, file sharing and group collaboration into social networking to create a rich environment for workplace collaboration. It breaks down the information silos caused by e-mail by facilitating free information flow across departments and hierarchy. Enterprise class security and access control features make sure that the company's data never reaches unintended people, either inside our outside the company.

Micro-blogging

Share ideas and work status with others. Get a continuous feed of activity from co-workers you follow.

Profiles

Know more about people around you with profiles that include expertise, contact details and a complete activity log.

Networking

Connect with people you are interested in, across teams and departments – Ask questions and get quick answers.

Groups

Create groups for your team to collaborate privately or create public groups for anyone to join and share.

Private Messaging

Start closed discussions with specific individuals. Add and remove people to conversations with all context intact.

Topics

Organize content in your network by tags. Subscribe to tags to get relevant content in your feed.

Sharing

Share files and manage revisions of files. Share links/videos with rich preview.

Polls

Create polls, participate in collaborative decision-making.

Access Control

Choose who has access to your content - specific people, groups or the entire company.

Secure

Only people with verified company email can join the network. Information sent over internet is always encrypted.

Directory Integration

(De-)Provision accounts through existing directory. Automatically sync employee information into the network.

Knowledge Discovery

All user content indexed for quick access to company's knowledge base.

Mobile Access

Stay connected with your network from your mobile devices.

Administrative Tools

Helpful tools for the owners to monitor & manage their network.
