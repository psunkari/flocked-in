Take a Tour of Flocked-in

Email is so yesterday. Flocked-in brings you a rich and effective form of communication, to help you get work done faster and better at office, and hence make you more productive. It also relieves you from email overload by providing an alternate communication mechanism. If you are already on Facebook or LinkedIn you can get-on with Flocked-in within minutes
