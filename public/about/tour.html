What is flocked.in?

flocked.in is a private social network within your company, that helps you collaborate better with your co-workers.

flocked.in revolutionizes communication at work place, with a much richer, easier and effective form of communication compared to email.

Micro-blogging

Share status information, links with people, ask questions.

Profiles

Rich user profiles of people with their experiences and expertise.

Networking

Connect with people you are interested in. Get updates from them and share stuff.

Groups

Form closed groups for your team or department for private collaboration.

Private Messaging

Send private message to people and have closed discussions.

Topics

Organize content in your network by Tags. Subscribe to interested Tags and be informed.

Access control

Define who can access content that you create. Share confidential information with specific people.

Secure

Only people with verified company email id can join the network. All communication happens over https.
